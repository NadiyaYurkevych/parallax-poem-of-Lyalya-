Lyalya.

I was sitting in the pipe.🛣️ It was cold and scary. 🌧️⛈️ But someone's kind hands, 🤲 They found me so in time.💞 They took me to their home, warmed me, and fed me.🏡 🔥🥫🍱 In addition, they gave me candies, and named Lyalya. 🍬🐱 I feel very well in my new home. 🏡 I play all day with Tutsik(rabbit) in the backyard. 🐰🌞 And when the night comes, and I want to sleep, 🌃 I run very quickly to Nadiya's room. 🛋️🙋 There, I will curl up under her side, 🐱👩‍💻 And I will take a sweet nap in the deep black night...🌌

The poem of Lyalya-kitty had been written by Nadiya Yurkevych.
